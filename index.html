My Story

Five years. Thousands of readings. One purpose.

✦

I didn't set out to become a tarot reader. I set out to understand myself — and the cards became the mirror that helped me do it.

For over five years I've been giving readings in Edinburgh and online, holding space for thousands of individuals from all walks of life. Students and executives. New mothers and those starting over. People who believed, and people who arrived as sceptics and left transformed.

What I've found, every single time, is the same truth: you already know the answer. The cards simply create the conditions for you to hear it.

"The cards don't predict your future. They reveal what's already alive within you — and invite you to choose."

My practice has two pillars. The first is tarot and oracle — ancient symbolic systems that speak the language of the unconscious. The second is the Law of Assumption: the teaching that your inner world, your assumptions about yourself and reality, creates your outer experience without exception.

These two things work together beautifully. Tarot shows you what's happening beneath the surface. The Law of Assumption shows you how to change it.

I'm based in Edinburgh and work with clients globally via video call. My background is rooted in Indian spiritual tradition, and I bring that depth — alongside years of study — into every session I hold.

What I Offer

My Approach

What I believe about this work

  • You are not broken. You are becoming. Every reading meets you exactly where you are.
  • Clarity is not a luxury — it's a necessity. You deserve to understand your own life.
  • The Law of Assumption is not wishful thinking. It is a precise inner technology that works when applied with consistency.
  • Tarot is a tool, not a prophecy. The future is not fixed. You are always in the driver's seat.
  • Transformation is available to everyone — regardless of background, belief, or how long you've been stuck.

Working Together
